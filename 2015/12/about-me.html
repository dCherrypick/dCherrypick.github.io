About Me

Thu, 24 Dec 2015


Hi there,

Thank you for visiting!

I'm Gewei Wang, a data-cherry picker -- I pick outliers from massive data like:

  • Customers who are most likely to subscribe a service
  • Products that match some target groups
  • Search results that are most likely to be clicked
  • Stocks that consist of a optimal portfolio
  • Fraud SMS senders

I bake cookies biweekly. What I've found in baking is:

  • recipes are simply algorithms
  • I often have to tune a recipe to get the optimal result, even though the recipe is from some classic cookbooks
  • baking is rewarding: I can enjoy cookies myself, and can easily share them with people as well
  • the world of baking is so rich and deep that I can always be excited about some new things

The common things of data-cherry picking and baking is the sheer pleasure of magic, the transformation of something into profitable data products or delicious cookies.
